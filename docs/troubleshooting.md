## Troubleshooting

Use this section to diagnose recurring issues quickly and apply reliable mitigation steps.

### Common Symptoms

- Navigation behavior does not match expected routes.
- Theme preference does not persist as expected.
- Section links or anchors do not scroll correctly.

### Diagnostic Steps

Verify browser console messages, inspect DOM IDs for headings, and confirm asset paths are valid.

### Escalation Path

If issues persist, collect reproducible steps and share them with maintainers through support channels.
